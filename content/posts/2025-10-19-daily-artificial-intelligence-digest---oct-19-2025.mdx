<h2>
  
  
  AI Governance &amp; Societal Impact
</h2>

<p>AI-powered surveillance technologies present significant ethical challenges, as evidenced by a Texas sheriff's misuse of <a href="https://www.techdirt.com/2025/10/17/flock-safety-texas-sheriff-claimed-license-plate-search-was-for-a-missing-person-it-was-an-abortion-investigation/" rel="noopener noreferrer">license plate reader data</a> from Flock Safety. Initially claimed for a missing person case, the search was later revealed to be part of an abortion investigation. This incident highlights the critical need for robust governance frameworks and transparent accountability to prevent the abuse of advanced AI systems in sensitive contexts, safeguarding civil liberties against potential overreach.</p>
