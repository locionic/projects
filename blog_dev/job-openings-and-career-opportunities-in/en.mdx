# Job Openings and Career Opportunities in Tech

Are you looking for a new challenge in the tech industry? Here are some exciting job openings and career opportunities to consider:

### Close: Site Reliability Engineer

Close is seeking a Site Reliability Engineer to help build and manage their sales communication platform. If you have experience with databases (SQL and NoSQL), infrastructure management, and backend services, this might be the perfect fit for you.

### Wynter: Research Panel Participants

Wynter is looking for system administrators, engineers, and developers to join their research panel. Participate in surveys, share your feedback, and earn $90-$180 per hour.

### Kinsta: DevOps Engineer, SysOps Engineer, and JavaScript Developer

Kinsta has three exciting job openings:

* **DevOps Engineer**: Help maintain and improve Kinsta's infrastructure, ensuring it remains stable and high-performing.
* **SysOps Engineer**: Care for the health of Kinsta's servers, prevent issues, and respond to problems in the infrastructure.
* **JavaScript Developer**: Join Kinsta's development team and focus on creating high-quality code for their customers and internal teams.

## Cool Products and Services

### G-Core Labs CDN

G-Core Labs offers a next-generation content delivery network (CDN) with built-in web security features and a rapidly growing Anycast/DNS infrastructure.

### Pinecone

Pinecone is a vector database that makes it easy to add semantic search to your applications. It combines state-of-the-art vector search libraries, advanced features, and fully managed distributed infrastructure.

### Bridgecrew

Bridgecrew is a cloud security platform for developers that leverages automation and delivers security-as-code to find, fix, and prevent misconfigurations in deployed cloud resources and infrastructure as code.

### IP2Location

IP2Location is an IP address geolocation service provider that detects location, proxy, and other parameters. Their technology has been cited in over 700 research papers and trusted by many Fortune 500 companies.

### StackHawk and Snyk

Learn how engineering teams are using products like StackHawk and Snyk to add security bug testing to their CI pipelines.

### FaunaDB

Effortlessly add state to your JavaScript apps with FaunaDB, which offers a generous free tier.

### Educative.io

Check out Educative.io's 5-part learning path: Scalability and System Design for Developers, which includes core material from their bestselling course Grokking the System Design Interview.

### getstream.io

Build, scale, and personalize your news feeds and activity streams with getstream.io, which offers a free API and client libraries for Node, Ruby, Python, PHP, Go, Java, and .NET.
