# Embracing Change: Cloudflare's Journey into the Agentic AI Era

Today, Cloudflare's leadership team shared a significant update with the company's global workforce. As part of our commitment to transparency, we want to share this news directly with our team members and the public.

## A New Era for Cloudflare

The way we work at Cloudflare has undergone a profound transformation. We're no longer just a company that builds and sells AI tools and platforms; we're also our own most demanding customer. Our usage of AI has increased by over 600% in the last three months alone, with thousands of AI agent sessions run daily by employees across various departments.

To thrive in this new landscape, we must intentionally architect our company for the agentic AI era. This means reimagining every internal process, team, and role to supercharge the value we deliver to our customers and honor our mission to build a better Internet for everyone, everywhere.

## A Difficult but Necessary Decision

Unfortunately, this transformation requires us to reduce our workforce by more than 1,100 employees globally. This decision was not made lightly, and we want to emphasize that it's not a reflection of the individual work or talent of those leaving us. Instead, we're redefining how a world-class, high-growth company operates and creates value in the agentic AI era.

## Supporting Departing Team Members

We're committed to treating departing team members with empathy and respect. We'll be providing industry-leading severance packages, including:

* Full base pay through the end of 2026
* Continued healthcare coverage through the end of the year (for US-based employees)
* Vesting of equity through August 15th
* Waiving one-year cliffs for departing team members who haven't reached this milestone

## Why We're Making These Changes Now

We're taking decisive action now to provide immediate clarity to those departing and protect the stability of the team that remains. By making these changes in one step, we avoid prolonged emotional uncertainty for employees and stalls our ability to build.

## Building for the Future

Cloudflare started as a digitally native company built in the cloud, allowing us to catch up to and pass companies with outdated systems and processes. As we've become the leader, we cannot rest on the workflows and organizational structures that worked yesterday. We're confident that our reshaped organization will be even faster and more innovative as we continue building the future.

## A Message to Departing Team Members

To those leaving us, we have the utmost respect for your work and gratitude for the impact you've made. We're confident you'll land at other great places and build many future great companies, bringing with you a unique set of skills learned while building Cloudflare.

## Next Steps

We'll be sharing more about today's announcements on our earnings conference call at 2 PM PT and addressing the team live at our all-hands meeting. It's not an easy day, but it's the right decision. Our mission to help build a better Internet is more important now than ever, and there's a lot of work left to be done.
