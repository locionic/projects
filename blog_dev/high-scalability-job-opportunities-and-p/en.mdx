# High Scalability Job Opportunities and Products

## Who's Hiring?

Several companies are looking for talented individuals to join their teams:

### Close

Close is seeking a Site Reliability Engineer to help build the sales communication platform of the future. The ideal candidate has a passion for databases (both SQL and NoSQL), significant experience building, managing, and monitoring infrastructure, databases, and backend services at scale.

### Wynter

Wynter is looking for system administrators, engineers, and developers to join its research panel. Participants will receive $90-$180/hr for their feedback and comments in research surveys, with a low commitment of 10-15 minutes per survey.

### Kinsta

Kinsta has several job openings:

* **DevOps Engineer**: Kinsta is looking for an experienced DevOps Engineer to ensure their infrastructure is always on the bleeding edge of technology, remaining stable and high-performing at all times.
* **SysOps Engineer**: As a SysOps Engineer at Kinsta, you'll play a key role in caring for the health of their servers, preventing issues, and responding immediately to mitigate any problems in their infrastructure.
* **JavaScript Developer**: Kinsta's Development Team is seeking a talented JavaScript developer to work on creating truly effective solutions for their customers and internal teams.

## Cool Products and Services

### G-Core Labs CDN

G-Core Labs offers a next-generation content delivery network (CDN) that helps accelerate dynamic and static content of websites or applications. Their CDN has many built-in web security features and operates through a rapidly growing Anycast/DNS infrastructure with over 140 points of presence worldwide.

### Pinecone

Pinecone is a vector database that makes it easy to add semantic search to applications. It combines state-of-the-art vector search libraries, advanced features such as filtering, and fully managed distributed infrastructure for high performance and reliability at any scale.

### Bridgecrew

Bridgecrew is a cloud security platform for developers that leverages automation and delivers security-as-code. It empowers teams to find, fix, and prevent misconfigurations in deployed cloud resources and infrastructure as code.

### IP2Location

IP2Location is an IP address geolocation service provider that detects location, proxy, and other parameters. Their technology has been cited in over 700 research papers and is trusted by many Fortune 500 companies.

### StackHawk

StackHawk is a tool that enables developers to take the lead on application security. It allows engineering teams to add security bug testing to their CI pipelines, ensuring the shipping of secure applications.

### FaunaDB

FaunaDB is a stateful JavaScript database that effortlessly adds state to JavaScript apps. It offers a generous free tier and is ideal for building scalable and secure applications.

### Educative.io

Educative.io offers a 5-part learning path on Scalability and System Design for Developers. This learning path includes core material from their bestselling course Grokking the System Design Interview and is designed to help developers balance architecture trade-offs and design scalable enterprise-level software.

### getstream.io

getstream.io is a platform that allows you to build, scale, and personalize news feeds and activity streams. It offers a free API, client libraries for various programming languages, and is used by over 400 companies for their production feed infrastructure.

### Triplebyte

Triplebyte is a platform that lets exceptional software engineers skip screening steps at hundreds of top tech companies. They offer a multiple-choice quiz (system design and coding questions) to help engineers scale their careers faster.
