# AI for the Rest of Us

If your eyes glaze over at jargon-filled AI articles, you're in the right place. This is a practical guide to tools you can use today—no hype, just productivity.

<Callout type="info" title="The Golden Rule">
  You don't need to understand neural networks. You just need to know what to type!
</Callout>

## Meet Your New Assistants

**ChatGPT**, **Claude**, and **Gemini** are conversational AIs. You ask a question in plain language, and they generate a response. Think of them as blazing-fast research assistants. 

<Stepper>
  <Step title="ChatGPT (OpenAI)">
    The generalist. Great at coding, creative writing, and handling a wide variety of tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claude (Anthropic)">
    The thoughtful one. Known for nuanced analysis, careful reasoning, and large context windows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gemini (Google)">
    The ecosystem player. Deeply integrated with Google and great at pulling real-time web info.
  </Step>
</Stepper>

## The Art of Prompting

You don't need a tutorial, just one simple principle: **the better your prompt, the better your result.**

<Callout type="idea" title="Level Up Your Prompts">
  Instead of saying *"Write about email"*, try *"Write a polite but firm email to my landlord about a repair that's been pending for three weeks."* Specificity is magic.
</Callout>

### Quick Tips for Success:
- **Set a Persona:** "I'm a beginner..." gets a better response than "Explain X."
- **Ask for Structure:** "Give me a pros and cons list."
- **Iterate:** If the first answer is off, follow up! It's a conversation.

## Honest Limitations

<Callout type="warning" title="AI is NOT Magic">
  - **Hallucinations:** AI can confidently make things up. Double-check facts!
  - **Bias:** AI learns from the internet, which isn't perfect.
  - **Context:** They don't know you. You have to provide the background.
</Callout>

Ready to start? Pick an AI, give it a small task (like drafting a dreaded email), and refine the results. You already know how to have a conversation—now just do it with your new digital assistant!
