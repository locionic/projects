# Optimizing Network Costs with Visa DCAP: Stripe's Solution

Stripe recently collaborated with Visa to integrate the Digital Commerce Authentication Program (DCAP) into its platform. This move enables businesses to minimize network costs while preserving high authorization rates. Here's an overview of the steps Stripe took to make this possible:

### Understanding the Challenge

The Visa DCAP is designed to help merchants reduce network costs by authenticating online transactions more efficiently. However, implementing this program requires technical expertise and infrastructure support. Stripe's goal was to simplify the process for its users, allowing them to focus on their core business operations.

### Stripe's Solution

To help businesses take advantage of the Visa DCAP, Stripe implemented the following measures:

* **Seamless Integration**: Stripe integrated the DCAP into its existing platform, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for its users.
* **Streamlined Authentication**: Stripe optimized its authentication processes to meet the DCAP's requirements, minimizing the need for manual intervention.
* **Interchange Savings**: By leveraging the DCAP, Stripe's users can now capture interchange savings on eligible transactions, reducing their overall network costs.
* **Authorization Rate Protection**: Stripe's solution prioritizes authorization rates, ensuring that businesses can maintain high approval rates while benefiting from the DCAP.

By taking these steps, Stripe has made it easier for businesses to optimize their network costs using the Visa DCAP. This integration demonstrates Stripe's commitment to providing innovative payment solutions that help its users succeed in a rapidly evolving digital commerce landscape.
