# TechPays Joins Forces with Levels.fyi to Advance Pay Transparency

Pay transparency has long been a pressing issue in the tech industry, particularly in Europe. The lack of reliable data on compensation packages often leads to misconceptions about salary ranges and hinders career growth. The story of TechPays, a pioneering European tech salary site, is a testament to the power of pay transparency and the impact it can have on individuals and the industry as a whole.

## The Genesis of TechPays

The idea for TechPays was born out of a personal experience. In 2016, while working as a principal engineer at Skyscanner in London, I was surprised to discover that my compensation could be significantly higher than what I was currently earning. An offer from Uber in Amsterdam effectively doubled my compensation, revealing a "secret, upper-tier" of the market that few knew existed.

This experience highlighted the need for reliable compensation data, particularly in Europe. I began collecting data points from over 200 engineers in the Netherlands and discovered a third, "hidden" tier of compensation. The findings were published in an article, "The Trimodal Nature of Software Engineering Salaries in the Netherlands and Europe."

## The Birth of TechPays

Following the success of the article, I decided to "open source" the compensation data points I collected and created TechPays with Zsombor Erdődy-Nagy. The site was designed to support compensation anonymization, capture freelancer compensation, and break down how compensation packages were structured. The response was overwhelming, with many users sharing stories of how TechPays helped them negotiate better compensation packages.

## A New Chapter for TechPays

As the years passed, both Zsombor and I became increasingly busy with other projects, and it became challenging to maintain TechPays. However, we were determined to find a way to keep the site running and ensure it received the care it deserved. After exploring various options, we are excited to announce that Levels.fyi will be taking over the operation of TechPays.

## Integrating with Levels.fyi

Levels.fyi is a global pay transparency platform that shares our vision of making compensation data accessible to everyone. The acquisition will integrate European compensation data into Levels.fyi's platform, providing users with a more comprehensive understanding of pay scales across the globe.

### What's Next for TechPays Users?

The acquisition will not affect the user experience on TechPays. You can continue to browse the data as before, and we expect even more high-quality data points to be added to the platform in the future. For more information on compensation data, please visit Levels.fyi.

### Additional Resources

To gain a deeper understanding of compensation trends, check out the article "The Trimodal Nature of Tech Compensation in the US, UK, and India," which is based on Levels.fyi data points.
