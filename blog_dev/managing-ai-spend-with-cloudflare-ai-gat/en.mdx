# Managing AI Spend with Cloudflare AI Gateway

The rapid adoption of AI technologies has left many companies struggling to manage their AI spend. With the pressure to innovate and stay ahead of the competition, companies have often prioritized speed over cost control. However, this approach can lead to unexpected and unsustainable expenses. Cloudflare is addressing this challenge with the introduction of spend controls and identity-driven budgets in its AI Gateway platform.

## The Problem of Uncontrolled AI Spend

The lack of visibility and control over AI spend can lead to a range of problems, including:

* **Unexpected expenses**: Without clear budgets and tracking, companies can be caught off guard by large and unexpected AI bills.
* **Inefficient resource allocation**: Without guidelines and controls, teams may default to using the most powerful and expensive AI models, even when smaller models would suffice.
* **Insufficient ROI analysis**: Without accurate cost attribution, companies cannot effectively calculate the return on investment (ROI) of their AI initiatives.

## Introducing AI Gateway Spend Controls

Cloudflare's AI Gateway platform now offers spend controls as a core feature. These controls enable companies to set budgets in dollars, track cumulative spend, and enforce limits on AI usage. The key features of spend controls include:

* **Budgeting**: Set budgets in dollars, not tokens, to track cumulative spend across all requests.
* **Scoping**: Scope limits to any combination of dimensions, including model, provider, or custom attributes like user, team, or application.
* **Windows**: Set fixed or rolling windows, with daily, weekly, or monthly limits.
* **Alerts**: Receive alerts when budget limits are reached (coming soon).

## Identity-Driven Budgets and Policies

In addition to spend controls, Cloudflare is introducing identity-driven budgets and policies as a closed beta. This feature enables companies to set per-user budgets, per-team model policies, and track cost attribution across the organization. The key features of identity-driven budgets and policies include:

* **Per-user budgets**: Set budgets for individual users, with options to downgrade or block requests when limits are reached.
* **Per-team model policies**: Set policies for teams, mapping directly to existing identity provider (IdP) groups.
* **Cost attribution**: Track cost attribution across the organization, with export options for analytics platforms.

## Getting Started with AI Gateway

To get started with AI Gateway, follow these steps:

1. **Create a gateway**: Point your applications at the AI Gateway endpoint.
2. **Set up spend limits**: Configure spend limits in the dashboard or via API.
3. **Monitor usage**: Start with a high limit in monitoring mode to understand current usage patterns.
4. **Sign up for the closed beta**: Apply for access to identity-driven budgets and policies.

## Conclusion

Cloudflare's AI Gateway platform offers a comprehensive solution for managing AI spend and optimizing costs. With spend controls and identity-driven budgets, companies can gain visibility and control over their AI expenses, ensuring a more sustainable and efficient approach to AI adoption.
