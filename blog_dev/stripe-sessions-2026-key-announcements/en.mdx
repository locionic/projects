# Stripe Sessions 2026: Key Announcements

At Sessions 2026, Stripe unveiled a range of exciting updates aimed at empowering businesses and developers. The announcements centered around three core themes: making Stripe more programmable, harnessing the strength of the Stripe network, and building economic infrastructure for AI.

### Programmability Enhancements

Stripe is committed to providing a more programmable platform, enabling developers to tailor the payment experience to their specific needs. By introducing new APIs, tools, and features, Stripe is making it easier for businesses to automate workflows, customize payment flows, and integrate with other services.

### Protecting and Propelling Businesses with Network Strength

The Stripe network is a powerful force in the world of payments, providing businesses with a robust and secure foundation for growth. By leveraging the collective strength of the network, Stripe is able to offer enhanced protection against fraud, improved payment success rates, and access to a broader range of payment methods.

### Building Economic Infrastructure for AI

As AI continues to transform industries and revolutionize the way businesses operate, Stripe is building the economic infrastructure to support this growth. By providing the necessary tools and platforms for AI-driven businesses, Stripe is empowering developers to create innovative new services and experiences that can reach a global audience.

These updates demonstrate Stripe's ongoing commitment to innovation and its dedication to supporting the growth and success of businesses and developers around the world.
