# Alternatives to Tailwind's Color Palette

When I decided to switch from Tailwind to vanilla CSS for new projects, I missed one feature in particular: the incredible, pre-defined color palette. Tailwind makes it effortless to choose a stunning shade.

However, moving away from Tailwind opened the door to explore beautiful, independent alternatives!

<Callout type="idea" title="Why Explore New Palettes?">
  Breaking away from standard frameworks gives your design a unique, tailored look that stands out from the sea of default Tailwind sites.
</Callout>

### More Design Systems & Generators

If you need something more dynamic, check out these excellent design systems and tools:

- **Web Awesome / Radix:** Fully-fledged design systems with built-in, meticulously tuned palettes.
- **Generators:** Harmonizer, Tints.dev, Coolors, and Colorpalette.pro are great for generating completely custom palettes, though they can have a slight learning curve.

<Callout type="info" title="Next-Gen Color: OKLCH">
  Want to dynamically generate colors right in your CSS? Look into **OKLCH**. It's a modern color model that ensures perceptual uniformity, making generative UI colors a breeze!
</Callout>
