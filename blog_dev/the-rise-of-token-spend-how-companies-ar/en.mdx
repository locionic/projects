# The Rise of Token Spend: How Companies are Managing the Cost of AI Tools

The increasing popularity of AI tools in software development has brought about a new challenge: managing the cost of token spend. In this article, we'll explore how companies of various sizes are dealing with the rapid growth of token spend and the strategies they're implementing to mitigate its impact on their budgets.

## The Problem of Token Spend

Token spend refers to the cost associated with using AI tools, such as language models and code generators, in software development. As more developers adopt these tools, the cost of token spend has increased significantly, breaking budgets and forcing companies to reevaluate their strategies.

## Large Companies

We spoke with developers and engineering leaders at several large companies, including a 10,000+ person SaaS company, a fintech company, and an infra company. Here's what we learned:

* **Setting default models to cheaper options**: Some companies are setting default models to cheaper options, such as Claude Sonnet, to reduce costs.
* **Monitoring and restricting usage**: Others are monitoring usage and restricting access to more expensive models to prevent overspending.
* **Guidance on model selection**: Some companies are offering guidance on model selection, such as recommending the use of cheaper models for certain tasks.

## Mid-Sized Companies

We also spoke with developers and engineering leaders at mid-sized companies, including a SaaS company and a finance company. Here's what we learned:

* **Model routing**: Some companies are using model routing to reduce costs by directing traffic to cheaper models.
* **Measuring impact**: Others are measuring the impact of AI tools on their business and adjusting their usage accordingly.
* **Blocking expensive models**: Some companies are blocking or restricting access to expensive models to prevent overspending.

## Small Companies

We spoke with developers and engineering leaders at several small companies, including a Series A startup and a seed-stage company. Here's what we learned:

* **Increasing AI budget**: Some companies are increasing their AI budget to accommodate the growing demand for AI tools.
* **Optimizing token consumption**: Others are optimizing token consumption by using cheaper models for simpler tasks and reviewing token usage.
* **Integrating more AI providers**: Some companies are integrating more AI providers to reduce dependence on a single vendor and negotiate better prices.

## Strategies for Managing Token Spend

Regardless of company size, there seem to be two strategies for managing token spend:

### Strategy #1: "Let it rip and start measuring"

* Allow developers to use AI tools as much as needed
* Measure usage and impact to understand the value of AI tools
* Adjust usage and budget accordingly

### Strategy #2: "Optimize and restrict"

* Optimize token consumption by using cheaper models and reviewing usage
* Restrict access to expensive models to prevent overspending
* Monitor and adjust usage to ensure alignment with business goals

In conclusion, the rapid growth of token spend is a challenge that companies of all sizes are facing. By understanding the strategies that companies are implementing to manage token spend, developers and engineering leaders can make informed decisions about how to use AI tools effectively and efficiently.
