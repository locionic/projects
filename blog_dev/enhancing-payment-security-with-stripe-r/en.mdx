# Enhancing Payment Security with Stripe Radar

Stripe Radar, a powerful tool for detecting and preventing payment fraud, has been expanded to provide even more comprehensive protection for businesses. The latest updates enable Radar to block high-risk transactions across all supported payment methods, ensuring that your business is safeguarded from potential threats.

### Key Enhancements

* **Multi-Payment Method Protection**: Radar now blocks high-risk transactions regardless of the payment method used, providing a unified layer of defense across your entire payment ecosystem.
* **Advanced Fraud Detection**: Radar defends against new and emerging fraud types, including:
	+ Multi-account abuse: Radar identifies and blocks suspicious activity across multiple accounts.
	+ Pay-as-you-go abuse: Radar detects and prevents abuse of pay-as-you-go services.
* **Universal Protection**: Radar's protection capabilities are not limited to Stripe's payment processing. It can be used to defend against fraud regardless of the payment processor used.
* **Platform Risk Evaluation and Mitigation**: Radar provides platforms with new tools to evaluate and mitigate merchant risk, both on and off Stripe. This enables platforms to make more informed decisions about the merchants they work with.

By expanding Radar's capabilities, Stripe is providing businesses with a more robust and comprehensive payment security solution. With Radar, businesses can focus on growth and innovation, knowing that their payments are protected from potential threats.
