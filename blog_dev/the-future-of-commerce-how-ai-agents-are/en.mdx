**What is Agentic Commerce?**

Agentic commerce refers to the integration of AI agents into e-commerce platforms, enabling more personalized, efficient, and automated shopping experiences. These agents can analyze customer behavior, optimize product recommendations, and streamline logistics, among other tasks.
